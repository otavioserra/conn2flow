## What is the Contacts Module?

The **Contacts** module stores and manages all inquiries received through your website's contact forms. It's your inbox for visitor messages, allowing you to view, respond to, and organize incoming communications.

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: I'm not receiving contact notifications
**A:** Check:
1. Admin email is configured correctly
2. Email notification is enabled
3. Check spam/junk folder
4. Verify email server settings

### Q: Contact form submissions aren't appearing
**A:** Verify:
1. Form is correctly configured
2. No validation errors for users
3. Check for spam blocking
4. Review system error logs

### Q: Can I reply to contacts from this system?
**A:** If configured, yes. Otherwise, copy the email and reply from your email client.

### Q: Are there spam contacts?
**A:** If you see spam:
1. Add CAPTCHA to your forms
2. Enable spam protection
3. Delete spam contacts
4. Consider honeypot fields

### Q: Can I restore deleted contacts?
**A:** No, deletion is permanent. Export regularly if you need records.

## üí° Best Practices

### Response Time
1. **Reply quickly** - Within 24-48 hours
2. **Set expectations** - Auto-reply with timeline
3. **Prioritize** - Star urgent messages
4. **Track** - Monitor response rates

### Organization
1. **Check regularly** - At least daily
2. **Clean up** - Delete old/spam contacts
3. **Export** - Backup important contacts
4. **Categorize** - Use stars for priority

### Form Optimization
1. **Required fields** - Only ask for essentials
2. **Clear labels** - Users know what to enter
3. **Confirmation** - Show success message
4. **Anti-spam** - Protect against bots
