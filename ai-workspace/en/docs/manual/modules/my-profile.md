## What is My Profile?

**My Profile** is where you manage your own account settings. Here you can update your personal information, change your password, and configure your preferences.

### How to Get There
1. Click your **name or avatar** in the top-right corner

### Updating Your Name
1. Click on the name field
2. Type your new name
3. Click **"Save"**

### Changing Your Email
1. Enter your new email address
2. You may need to confirm via email
3. Click **"Save"**

## üì∏ Profile Picture (Avatar)

### Uploading a New Avatar
1. Click on your current avatar or the **"Change"** button
2. Select an image from your computer
3. The image will be automatically resized
4. Click **"Save"**

### Image Requirements
- Formats: JPG, PNG
- Maximum size: 2MB
- Recommended: Square image (will be cropped to square)

## üîê Changing Your Password

### Step by Step
1. Go to the **Security** section
2. Enter your **current password**
3. Enter your **new password**
4. Enter the new password again to **confirm**
5. Click **"Change Password"**

### Password Tips
- Use at least 8 characters
- Mix uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include numbers and symbols
- Don't use personal information (birthdays, names)
- Don't reuse passwords from other sites

## üì± Active Sessions

### What Are Sessions?
Sessions represent devices where you're logged in. You can see:
- Device type (computer, phone)
- Browser used
- Location (if available)
- When the session started

### Managing Sessions
- **Current session** - Marked as "This device"
- **End a session** - Click "Log out" next to any session
- **End all sessions** - Log out from all devices at once

> üí° **Tip:** If you see a session you don't recognize, end it immediately and change your password!

## üîí Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

If your system has 2FA enabled:

### Enabling 2FA
1. Go to **Security** section
2. Click **"Enable 2FA"**
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
4. Enter the code from the app to confirm
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place!

### Using 2FA
After enabled, when logging in:
1. Enter your email and password
2. Open your authenticator app
3. Enter the 6-digit code

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: I forgot my password
**A:** Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, or ask your administrator to reset it.

### Q: Can I change my username?
**A:** Your username is your email address. You can change it in the Personal Information section.

### Q: My avatar isn't showing
**A:** Try:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Upload a smaller/different image

### Q: I can't access My Profile
**A:** This feature might be disabled by your administrator. Contact them for help.
