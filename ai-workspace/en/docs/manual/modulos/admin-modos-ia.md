## What are AI Modes?

**AI Modes** define different "personalities" or behaviors for AI-powered features. Each mode tells the AI how to respond - formal, creative, technical, or custom styles.

## ‚ûï Creating a Mode

### How to Create
1. Click **"Add Mode"**
2. Fill in:
   - **Name** - Descriptive name
   - **System Prompt** - Instructions for AI
   - **Temperature** - Creativity level (0-1)
   - **Description** - What it's for
3. Click **"Save"**

### System Prompt Tips
```
Good: "You are a professional business writer. Use formal language, 
      clear structure, and avoid jargon."

Better: "You are a professional business writer for a tech company.
        Write in a formal but approachable tone. Structure content
        with clear headings. Avoid technical jargon unless necessary.
        Target audience: business executives."
```

- **Technical docs**: 0.1 - 0.3

## ‚ùì FAQ

### Q: Which mode should I use?
**A:** Match the mode to your content type:
- Email ‚Üí Professional
- Blog post ‚Üí Creative
- Help docs ‚Üí Technical

### Q: Can I have multiple modes active?
**A:** Yes, switch between modes as needed.

### Q: My mode produces wrong results
**A:** Review and refine your system prompt. Be more specific about expectations.
