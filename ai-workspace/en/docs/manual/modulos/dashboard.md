### Using the Search Bar
The Dashboard has a convenient search feature to quickly find modules:

1. **Locate the search bar** in the top-right corner of the Dashboard
2. **Type your search** - you can search by:
   - Module name (e.g., "Users", "Pages")
   - Description keywords
   - Category name
3. **Cards filter automatically** as you type
4. **Click the reset button** (X) to clear your search and show all cards again

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: I can't find a module I'm looking for
**A:** Try these steps:
1. Clear the search field and browse all categories
2. Check with your administrator - you may not have permission to access that module
3. The module might be disabled or hidden

### Q: Some cards are grayed out
**A:** You don't have permission to access those modules. Contact your administrator if you need access.

**A:** Go to **Modules** in the administration section to reorder modules.
