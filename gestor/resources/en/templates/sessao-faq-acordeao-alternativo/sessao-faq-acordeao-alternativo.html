Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to your questions about our services and discover how we can help your business.

How does the free trial work?
Our trial period is completely free and gives access to all premium features for 14 days. No credit card is required to start, and you can cancel anytime without cost.
Can I change plans later?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time directly from the dashboard. Upgrade changes are applied immediately, and downgrades take effect in the next billing cycle.
Do you offer technical support?
We offer support via chat and email for all plans. Enterprise plan clients have 24/7 priority support and a dedicated account manager.
Are my data secure?
We take security very seriously. We use AES-256 encryption for all stored data and TLS for data in transit. Your backups are performed automatically every day.
